Introducing Radon

Online journaling, focused on security and simplicity

What is it?

With Radon, you can create daily entries to follow up on your projects, life, ideas, ... pretty much anything you want. It uses zero-knowledge end-to-end encryption, so you know you can speak freely :relaxed:

Another journaling app?

I used to be a big fan of OhLife (RIP). Every day, I'd write a quick summary of the stuff a got done, and what I was planning on doing next. One thing made me feel uneasy though: what if the site ever got breached? So I decided to try and create my own, zero-knowledge journal. That way, even if the database ever got hacked, I could sleep easy knowing it would only contain unreadable gibberish.
